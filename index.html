UX DRIVEN WEBSITES, CUSTOMIZED WITH CARE

Alpha Design & Development is a web development firm based in Sarasota, Florida that specializes in UX driven websites, e-commerce sites, portals, blogs and all aspects of web development. Our web development studio was founded in 2014 by a team with over 10 years experience in web development.

We offer creative digital solutions and fresh & creative ideas. We take pride in our work and everything we create is executed with precision and love. We build websites that revolve around user experience (UX) and usability, while keeping true to the very last pixel of the selected design.

Those unique aspects along with our creativity, experience and knowledge guarantee the highest quality in our work.

4 REASONS WHY...

WE OFFER FUNCTIONAL SOLUTIONS, FRESH & CREATIVE IDEAS.

01

Custom Built

Every project is unique and has its own objectives. There is not a one-formula-for-all. We adapt our development process to each design and project needs. We rely on the right tools and make our own code. We do not use templates. With no restrictions we can transform every design into a website.

02

Made with Love

We love what we do. For us every new design is a challenge to be creative and make new wonderful things! We create pixel perfect websites and the easiest-to-use Content Management System (CMS), that always fits the project needs.

03

Latest Technologies

Being creative is not enough. Web developers need to keep up with new technologies and trends. That’s what we do. We research, test, evaluate and use the technologies that best fit each project. This way we gain new perspectives, the ability to make better animations, build lighter & faster websites that are friendly to search engines. The code, itself, is very important to have the correct form and follow specific rules with semantic markup that also assist the SEO.

04

Compatibility

It is the way each browser or device renders and supports a website. A contemporary website needs to load correctly, without errors, in all modern browsers. Additionally, there is a new trend, called responsive design, that makes a website adjustable to every device and screen size.
