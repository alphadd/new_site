9 Four One Media

Alpha Design & Development

RESPONSIVE WEBSITE

9 Four One Media is a Berlin based international curated platform aiming to introduce, promote and connect artists from Europe to the creative market of Asia. The platform is populated with top artists and agencies in many different areas.

The website’s need was to promote the platform but also its members along with their projects.

The result is a responsive website that adapts to all screen resolutions, ranging from a desktop computer to a mobile phone.
